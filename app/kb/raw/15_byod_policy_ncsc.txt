Title: BYOD – Secure Personal Devices at Work
Citation: (NCSC, 2023)
URL: https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/guidance/bring-your-own-device-byod

Summary:
Bring Your Own Device policies balance usability and risk. {cite} advises mobile device management (MDM) or app protection policies, enforced screen locks and encryption, separation of work and personal data, approved app stores, and rapid remote‑wipe for lost devices. Communicate clearly what the organisation can and cannot see to maintain trust.

Tags: byod,mobile,mdm,policy,uk
Audience: All employees, Managers
