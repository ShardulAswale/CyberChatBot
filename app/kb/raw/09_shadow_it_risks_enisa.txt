Title: Shadow IT – Risks and Controls
Citation: (ENISA, 2023)
URL: https://www.enisa.europa.eu/

Summary:
Shadow IT refers to unapproved tools or services used outside established governance. {cite} recommends inventorying cloud usage, establishing an approval process, conducting DPIAs where appropriate, and offering secure, user‑friendly alternatives. Communication is key: teams often choose shadow tools to overcome friction—address needs and provide sanctioned solutions rather than relying solely on prohibition.
