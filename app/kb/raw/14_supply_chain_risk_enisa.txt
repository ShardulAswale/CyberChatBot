Title: Supply‑Chain Cybersecurity
Citation: (ENISA, 2021)
URL: https://www.enisa.europa.eu/publications/threat-landscape-for-supply-chain-attacks

Summary:
Recent incidents show attackers target suppliers to reach customers. {cite} recommends integrating security criteria into procurement, assessing supplier controls, defining incident notification clauses, and monitoring third‑party access. Maintain a register of critical suppliers and dependencies, and plan for disruption scenarios (e.g., rapid revocation of access, replacement vendors).
