Title: GDPR Security Outcomes (NCSC + ICO)
Citation: (NCSC, 2024)
URL: https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/information/gdpr

Summary:
{cite} outlines practical security outcomes that align with GDPR’s principles: ensure confidentiality, integrity and availability; manage risks proportionately; protect against known threats; monitor and respond to incidents; manage suppliers; and train staff. The focus is on demonstrable accountability—showing that controls, processes, and records support lawful and secure processing.
