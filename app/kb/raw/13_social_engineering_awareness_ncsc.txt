Title: Social Engineering – Recognise and Resist
Citation: (NCSC, 2024)
URL: https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/

Summary:
Attackers manipulate trust and authority to elicit actions or information. {cite} advises verifying identities through independent channels, using visitor controls to prevent tailgating, and challenging unusual requests—even from senior staff. Reporting attempts helps the organisation tune controls and raise awareness. Cultivate a positive reporting culture rather than blame.
