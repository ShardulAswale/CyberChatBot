Title: Cloud Security – Basics and Shared Responsibility
Citation: (NCSC, 2023)
URL: https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/collection/cloud

Summary:
Cloud adoption shifts some responsibilities to providers, but customers retain duties for identity, access, and data protection. {cite} recommends understanding shared‑responsibility models, using strong identity and MFA, encrypting data at rest and in transit, and monitoring for misconfiguration. Review supplier security, locations of data processing, and exit strategies.
