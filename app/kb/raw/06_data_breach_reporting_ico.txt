Title: Personal Data Breaches – Reporting and Timing
Citation: (ICO, 2024)
URL: https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/report-a-breach/personal-data-breaches-a-guide/

Summary:
Under UK GDPR, a personal data breach involves accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure of, or access to, personal data. {cite} requires controllers to assess risk to individuals’ rights and freedoms and, if risk is likely, notify the ICO without undue delay and within 72 hours where feasible. Notifications must describe the nature, scope, likely consequences, and measures taken. Maintain an internal breach log and follow escalation procedures in your incident plan.
